TURN SEQUENCE
On your turn, you'll perform the following three actions:
  1. 1 Place a building tile on its matching space.
  2. 2 Buy up to three stocks in any active hotel chains from the stock market tray.
  3. 3 Draw a new building tile from the facedown pile.
